Lifelong artist and learner. Currently learning web development. I learned layout and keylining
in a night class at ECC. I got an art assistant job just after that at Delta Publishing. A few years

QuarkXPress?) when I found that if I knew it, I would have a job on Monday.
Over the long weekend I read the entire Quark manual. I got the job and was employed there for a couple of years.
